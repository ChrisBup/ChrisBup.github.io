ABOUT


I recently graduated from the Web Development Immersive (WDI) program at General Assembly, a 12-week full-time dev bootcamp and an incredible experience.


For me, development and design come down to organizing information so that it's most accessible to the user; i.e. conveying a message efficiently and crafting an intuitive experience.


When I'm not coding, I like to run while listening to podcasts, play pick-up soccer, cook mediterranean and pet any dog I meet.
I keep visual journals where I collect design ideas, inspiration and make all sorts of lists.


I love lists.


But basically, I'm a sponge for all things web development so if you have any advice or questions or are looking for a rookie coder who's weirdly enthusiastic about RESTful routes, closures and database queries, please reach out!
